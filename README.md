## ✨ Everything you watch, in one app

<table>
<tr>
<td width="50%" valign="top">

📺 **Live TV** — your IPTV playlists with a full program guide (EPG), favorites &amp; channel search

⏪ **DVR / Timeshift** — pause, rewind, fast-forward, and snap back to live on any channel

🎬 **Movies &amp; Series** — browse, search, and play your VOD library with rich metadata

</td>
<td width="50%" valign="top">

🟣 **Twitch** · 🟢 **Kick** · 🔴 **Rumble** — your favorite streamers, with chat, in the app

🔲 **MultiView** — watch up to 4 streams at once, across providers

⚡ **Native &amp; fast** — hardware-accelerated video (mpv + D3D11), built with Rust + Tauri

</td>
</tr>
